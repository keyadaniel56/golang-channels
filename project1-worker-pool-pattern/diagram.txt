Key Concepts Demonstrated:
• Fan-out: Multiple workers reading from same channel
• Synchronization: WaitGroup ensures all workers complete
• Channel Direction: taskCh (<-chan), resultCh (chan<-)
• Buffered Channels: Allows non-blocking sends
• Graceful Shutdown: Closing channels to signal completion
